History

Master planned back in the mid-1970s, ground was broken on what is now the Green Valley community in the spring of 1985. What started out as a single community has grown to encompass four zip codes, 9 elementary schools, 4 middle schools, 2 high schools, and more than a dozen private academies, not to mention hundress of local businesses and large chains.

World Class Casinos

Green Valley is home to two of the valley's premier Off-The-Strip casinos, The Sunset Station and the Ranch at Green Valley. Both provide more than just great casinos, but also boast several of Las Vegas' best staidum-style movie theaters, two amazing bowling alleys, and some of the best food courts in Nevada.
