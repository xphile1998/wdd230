Target Audience

The target audience for this website is as descibed below:

  • Who: Small business owners and people living in the Green Valley community in Henderson, Nevada.
  • Age: Anyone from 1 to 100!
  • Technology: We aim to get people involved through the website, email, and face to face interactions!
  • Income: Anyone and any price.
  • Motivation: The Green Valley Chamber of Commerce seeks to improve the relations between the Green Valley community and the small business owners living and working in this community.

Scenarios

  1. How can I improve my business standing in the community?
  2. What events are coming up?
  3. How do I find out how to do business in the Green Valley area?
  4. Where can I go to find a business mentor?
  5. Where can I find out what small businesses are operating in Green Valley?

Content List

This section outlines how each of the scenarios above might be met through the content in this website.

Scenario 1: How can I improve my business standing in the community?

  • Text explaining how the chamber works and what guidence they provide for business owners.
  • Catalogue of training materials available for download or through the mail.
  • Page of real world examples of how the CoC has assisted other small business succeed in Green Valley.

Scenario 2: What events are coming up?

  • A calendar of upcoming events which is clickable to zoom in on details for that event.
  • Search window to help business owners and community members find events.
  • Email form to request information on signing up for events.

Scenario 3: How do I find out how to do business in the Green Valley area?

  • Page explaining the Green Valley CoC Business School program
  • Catalogue of workshops and classes on how to start, manage, and grow a business in Green Valley.
  • Page form to sign up for the program.

Scenario 4: Where can I go to find a business mentor?

  • Page explaining the mentorship program and how it works.
  • List of the business mentors in the area.
  • Page form to sign up for the program.

Scenario 5: Where can I find out what small businesses are operating in Green Valley?

  • Searchable index of all the small businesses in the area
  • Can search by type of business, business name, whether they are a member of the CoC, etc.
  • Contact This Business form to make asking question easier.
